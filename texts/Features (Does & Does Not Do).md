# What HR Leave Assist (HRLA) Does / Does Not Do

| HRLA Does | HRLA Does Not |
| :--- | :--- |
| Provides clear, practical guidance on employee leave and accommodation questions under **California and Federal law** | Provide legal advice or medical advice |
| Translates complex leave frameworks (e.g., CFRA, PDL, FMLA, ADA) into understandable, HR-ready explanations | Make final eligibility or compliance determinations |
| Helps HR professionals understand how different leave and accommodation rules may apply to a specific scenario | Replace legal counsel, medical certification, or agency determinations |
| Supports consistent, compliant communication with employees | Draft policies, handbooks, or formal legal documents |
| Identifies when the **interactive process** or accommodation considerations may be required | Conduct or document the interactive process on your behalf |
| Flags overlapping or concurrent leave considerations | Guarantee compliance or risk-free outcomes |
| Uses jurisdiction-specific logic based on the **selected state or federal scope** | Compare laws across states or provide multi-state analysis |
| Encourages appropriate follow-up questions when information is missing or unclear | Assume facts, make medical judgments, or interpret diagnoses |
| Reflects laws generally in effect **as of the date shown in the tool** | Monitor or automatically update for real-time law changes |
| Helps HR teams respond more quickly and confidently to routine leave questions | Store or manage employee medical records or sensitive personal data |

---

### Plain-Language Summary for Buyers
HRLA helps you understand the rules and apply them thoughtfully. You remain responsible for the final decision.
